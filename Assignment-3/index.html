Digital Addiction – A Modern Day Challenge

Digital addiction is the excessive use of smartphones, social media, gaming, and online platforms that negatively affects physical health, mental well-being, and productivity. Continuous exposure to screens can lead to sleep disorders, reduced concentration, anxiety, and social isolation.
