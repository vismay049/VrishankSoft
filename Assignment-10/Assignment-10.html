About Krishna Mutt

Krishna Mutt is one of the most sacred pilgrimage sites in South India, founded by Sri Madhvacharya in the 13th century. The temple is famous for the unique Kanakana Kindi window through which devotees view Lord Krishna. Daily rituals and festivals attract thousands of devotees every year.

About Malpe Beach

Malpe Beach is a scenic coastal destination offering boating, water sports, and breathtaking sunset views. It is one of the cleanest beaches in Karnataka and serves as a gateway to St. Mary’s Island.

Top Nearby Places to Visit

St. Mary's Island

Kaup Beach & Lighthouse
Offers panoramic sea views from the historic lighthouse tower.

Delta Beach
A serene confluence point where the river meets the Arabian Sea.

Maravanthe Beach
One of India’s most scenic highways where the road runs between river and sea.

Local Cuisine

Udupi is globally famous for its vegetarian cuisine. Must-try dishes include: Masala Dosa, Goli Baje, Sambar, Rasam, and traditional Udupi Thali.

Best Time to Visit

October to March is the best season to explore Udupi due to pleasant weather. Monsoon season offers lush greenery but heavy rainfall.

Travel Tips

• Dress modestly when visiting temples.
• Carry sunscreen for beach visits.
• Early morning is ideal for temple darshan.
• Try local restaurants for authentic Udupi meals.
